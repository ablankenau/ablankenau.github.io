About Me

I am a junior at Occidental College pursuing a major in Psychology and a minor in Gender, Women, & Sexuality Studies. In Dr. Andrea Hopmeyer's Social-Developmental Psychology lab, I am currently leading a research study examining reasons for loneliness among emerging adults in college with a focus on gender- and sexual- minority students. On campus, I work as a Writing Adviser in Occidental's Writing Center where I help students improve their writing skills in collaborative 30 minute sessions at any stage in the writing process. After Occidental, I plan to attend graduate school to pursue a PhD in Counseling Psychology. My career goals are to become a licensed Psychologist, therapist, and researcher who is accessible to the LGBTQIA+ community, low-income and communities of color, and international adoptees.
